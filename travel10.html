MAKANDAWA RAINFOREST TREK Available Now

Immerse yourself in the breathtaking beauty of Makandawa Rainforest with our guided trekking experience. This journey takes you through lush jungle trails, past cascading waterfalls, and into the heart of one of Sri Lanka's most biodiverse ecosystems. Our knowledgeable guides will introduce you to the rainforest's flora and fauna while sharing insights about conservation and local ecology. Suitable for nature lovers of all fitness levels, this trek offers a perfect blend of adventure and environmental education.

Trekking Highlights

  • Guided trek through pristine Makandawa Rainforest
  • Visit to secluded waterfalls with swimming opportunities
  • Wildlife spotting including endemic birds and monkeys
  • Interpretation of rainforest ecology by expert guides
  • Multiple trail options for different fitness levels
  • Small group sizes for personalized attention
  • Traditional Sri Lankan packed lunch included

Our Makandawa trek begins with a briefing at our base camp, where you'll receive an overview of the day's route and safety information. The trek progresses through varying terrain, from well-maintained paths to more adventurous jungle trails. Along the way, your guide will point out interesting plant species, animal tracks, and bird calls. The highlight for many is reaching one of the rainforest's hidden waterfalls, where you can cool off with a refreshing swim in crystal-clear pools. We provide a delicious packed lunch to enjoy in a scenic jungle clearing before completing the circuit back to base.

We offer different route options to suit various fitness levels, from gentle 2-hour walks to more challenging 5-hour treks. All routes are led by experienced guides who are trained in first aid and familiar with the rainforest's many trails. We provide basic trekking poles upon request and recommend sturdy footwear for the sometimes muddy and uneven terrain. This experience is perfect for families, solo travelers, and groups looking to connect with Sri Lanka's incredible natural heritage.

Safety Information

Your safety is our priority. Our guides carry first aid kits and are trained in wilderness first response. The trails are regularly maintained, but rainforest conditions can be unpredictable with occasional slippery or uneven surfaces. We monitor weather conditions and may modify routes if necessary. While wildlife encounters are generally safe, we maintain appropriate distances and your guide will advise on any precautions. Drinking water is provided, and we recommend bringing sun protection and insect repellent.

What to Bring

  • Sturdy walking shoes or hiking boots
  • Lightweight, breathable clothing (long sleeves/pants recommended)
  • Rain jacket (during rainy season)
  • Sun protection (hat, sunscreen)
  • Insect repellent
  • Swimwear and towel (for waterfall swims)
  • Small backpack for personal items
  • Camera/binoculars (optional)
  • Reusable water bottle (we provide refills)

Frequently Asked Questions

How difficult is the trekking?
We offer routes suitable for various fitness levels. The short trek (2-3 hours) involves gentle terrain with few steep sections, suitable for most people including families with children. The standard trek (4-5 hours) includes some moderate climbs and uneven terrain. The full day adventure is more challenging with longer distances and varied terrain. Our guides will help you choose the most appropriate route based on your fitness level and interests.
What wildlife might we see?
Makandawa is home to diverse wildlife including several endemic bird species (like Sri Lanka grey hornbill and layard's parakeet), purple-faced langur monkeys, giant squirrels, and various reptiles and amphibians. While we can't guarantee sightings, our guides know the best spots and times for wildlife viewing. Early morning treks typically offer the best wildlife viewing opportunities.
What happens if it rains?
Trekking in light rain can be a magical experience in the rainforest, and we provide rain ponchos if needed. However, in case of heavy rain or thunderstorms, we may delay the start time, modify the route, or reschedule for safety reasons. You'll be informed of any changes, and we offer full refunds if rescheduling isn't possible.
Is this suitable for children?
Absolutely! The short trek is perfect for families with children aged 5+. Our guides are experienced with families and know how to make the trek engaging for young explorers. We can adjust the pace and include more breaks as needed. Children under 8 can join the short trek for free when accompanied by an adult.
